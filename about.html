My Life

I love summer and late May. Nothing can be better than lazy sunny days near the sea. The only one thing I really like in winter is ice-skating. I skate because it is my passion. It challenges me both mentally and physically and allows me to express myself in many ways. I like the wind in my face and swoosh sound of my skates. I feel free on the ice and it is fun to learn something new. I skate simply because I love it! In my free time I do a lot of activities. Some of them are sports. For example, I like cycling around the neighbourhood, playing badminton with my friends and jogging round the park.
